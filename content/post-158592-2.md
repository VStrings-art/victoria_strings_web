# 158592

## Professional Violin

This violin displays a harmonious and elegant aesthetic, crafted from beautifully figured tonewoods. The two-piece maple back shows a striking, book-matched flame with consistent horizontal figure, enhanced by a warm golden-brown varnish that brings depth and luminosity to the wood.

The spruce top continues this refined appearance, finished in a smooth amber-to-antique-brown shading that reveals the natural grain beneath.

The scroll is confidently shaped with crisp chamfers and a honey-brown varnish that highlights its natural flame. Precise workmanship in the pegbox and smooth-fitting pegs underscore both the craftsmanship and functionality of the instrument.
